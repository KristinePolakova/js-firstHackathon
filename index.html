Unique approach for treatment under supervision of a Bird specialist

There are three different approaches to Parrot Therapy, please see more details under the section Pricing.

Therapy includes activities (such as caring for, feeding, keeping, and directing a parrot) that are supervised by a psychiatrist, often with the support of a bird professional. Both during and after the activity, the bird therapist can observe and interact with the patient to identify patterns of behavior and process thoughts and emotions. The goal of bird therapy is to help the patient develop the necessary skills and attributes such as responsibility, self-confidence, problem-solving skills, and self-control. Parrot therapy also provides an innovative environment in which the therapist and patient can identify and address a range of emotional and behavioral challenges
